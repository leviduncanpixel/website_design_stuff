Eleven46 is a Belgian company specialised in making fashionable socks. The designs are made at their Brussels HQ and made with the finest precision in Italy. They are still pretty young. They are available in stores and online on www.eleven46.co. Eleven46 refers to the smallest and largest size in socks (standard sizes). That's why we found it original to take that name. We tried many other options but this one sounded the best. We knew it was going to be challenging but people are liking the name more and more.
